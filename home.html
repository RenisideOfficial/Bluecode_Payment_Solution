What's New on Bluecode?

Bluecode is a mobile contactless payment, on the account rails of local financial institutions.
No need for a card, or card schemes.

Viva.com and Bluecode have partnered, allowing merchants to accept Bluecode QR payments via
the Viva.com platform.

Bluecode is a mobile payment system that utilizes barcode and QR code scanning,
as well as NFC technology, for contactless payments.

Bluecode has created an innovative solution that combines 40+ local payment networks into the
largest global payment network.

has recently expanded to include NFC (Near Field Communication) payments, enabling users to
tap their smartphones at payment terminal.
